**Vrinda Store Annual Report 2022**

Welcome to the Vrinda Store Data Analysis project! This repository contains a comprehensive data analysis of Vrinda Store's annual sales for the year 2022.
The goal of this project was to dive deepinto the sales data, understand customer behavior, and extract actionable business insights to help drive future growth.

**Dataset Overview**

The dataset used for this analysis includes a wide range of information about orders, customers, and product details. Here is a breakdown of the columns included 
in the dataset:

1. Index: Unique row identifier.
2. Order ID: Unique identification number for each order.
3. Customer ID: Unique identification number for each customer.
4. Gender: Gender of the customer (Men/Women).
5. Age Group: Categorization of customers by age (e.g., Teenager, Adult, Senior).
6. Age: Specific age of the customer.
7. Date: The date the order was placed.
8. Month: The month of the transaction.
9. Status: Current status of the order (e.g., Delivered, Refunded, Cancelled).
10. Channel: The sales platform or channel used (e.g., Amazon, Myntra, Ajio).
11. SKU: Stock Keeping Unit for the product.
12. Category: Product category (e.g., Kurta, Set, Western Dress).
13. Size: Size of the purchased item.
14. Qty: Quantity of items ordered.
15. Currency: Currency used for the transaction.
16 Amount: Total order value.
17. Ship City: City where the order was shipped.
18. Ship State: State where the order was shipped.
19. Ship Postal Code: Zip code of the shipping address.
20. Ship Country: Country where the order was shipped.
21. B2B: Indicates if the order was a Business-to-Business transaction.
    
**Key Insights**

Through this analysis, several interesting trends and patterns were uncovered:
Customer Demographics: The adult age group (between 30 and 90 years old) contributes to the maximum sales, making up for about 50% of the total revenue.
Purchasing Behavior: Women are significantly more likely to buy from the store compared to men.
Top Geographies: The top three states contributing to the sales are Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh.

## Key Business Insights

### Customer Demographics Analysis

* Female customers contributed approximately **64% of total sales**, significantly outperforming male customers and representing the primary revenue-generating customer segment.
* Customers within the **30–50 years age group** accounted for the highest share of purchases, highlighting this demographic as the most valuable target audience.

### Regional Performance Analysis

* **Maharashtra** emerged as the highest revenue-generating state, followed by **Karnataka** and **Uttar Pradesh**.
* These regions demonstrated strong sales performance and represent key markets for future business expansion and targeted marketing initiatives.

### Sales Channel Performance

* **Amazon** was identified as the leading sales channel, contributing the largest share of overall revenue.
* Flipkart and Myntra also delivered substantial sales volumes, emphasizing the importance of e-commerce platforms in driving business growth.

### Order Fulfillment Analysis

* The majority of customer orders were successfully delivered, reflecting efficient order management and fulfillment processes.
* Return, refund, and cancellation rates remained comparatively low, indicating strong customer satisfaction and operational effectiveness.

### Seasonal Sales Trends

* Sales performance peaked during the first quarter, particularly in **February and March**, suggesting the presence of seasonal demand patterns.
* A gradual decline in sales was observed during the latter half of the year, creating opportunities for promotional campaigns and customer engagement strategies.

### Strategic Recommendations

* Prioritize marketing efforts toward women customers aged 30–50 years, who represent the most profitable customer segment.
* Strengthen market penetration in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh through region-specific campaigns.
* Expand partnerships and promotional activities on high-performing e-commerce platforms, particularly Amazon and Flipkart.
* Implement targeted retention strategies to further reduce cancellations and returns while improving customer loyalty.
